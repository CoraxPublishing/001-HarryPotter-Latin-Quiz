Frequently Asked Questions

Does it cost to use your materials?

No subscriptions and no fees. All videos and interactive quizzes are free to access.

Want to help? Please like, share, and subscribe on our channels (YouTube, Facebook, etc.). It makes a real difference.

Optional purchases include companion e-books and print editions available via Amazon Kindle. You can also support our work on Patreon, and our Offers page announces monthly free Kindle e-book promotions for email subscribers.

How do you support independent authors?

We periodically feature independent authors whose work aligns with our focus—original Latin stories, resources for learners of Latin and English, and topics such as Mythology and the Classical world. We may also consider Tolkien-style fantasy, romance with classical themes, and political satire when the fit is right. Featured authors receive a brief bio and links on our platform.

How can I be featured (link and author bio)?

Send a short query via our Contact page or email ravencorax@protonmail.com. Include a summary, intended audience/level, and sample pages or a link to your published work. We aim to reply within 30 days. Final selection depends on editorial fit and availability.

Do I need previous experience?

No. Our materials serve absolute beginners through advanced learners. Latin videos use Latin narration with friendly English support; ESL videos are in clear English. Everything is built around learning by context.

What makes your approach different?

We teach through a comic / graphic-novel format—panels, speech bubbles, and vivid scenes—paired with the natural method (learn through stories and context). Every lesson includes an interactive MCQ to check understanding immediately.

Who are your resources for?

Teachers, students, and self-learners. Our materials work well in classrooms, study groups, and independent study.

Can I use your videos and quizzes in my class?

Yes—feel free to link or stream them for educational use. For printing, redistributing, or adapting our materials, please contact us for permission.

Where can I get your books?

Companion e-books and print editions are available on Amazon Kindle (links provided on our site). We announce monthly free Kindle e-book offers to first-time email subscribers.

How do I stay updated?

Subscribe to our newsletter on the site, check the Offers page, or follow our announcements. Patreon supporters also receive updates.

Technical requirements

Any modern browser on desktop or mobile. Audio is recommended for narrated videos. No account is required to watch videos or take quizzes.
