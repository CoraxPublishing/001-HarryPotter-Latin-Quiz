Mission Statement

Ave, sodales! We create welcoming, story-first learning for Latin and English through a comic book / graphic novel style—panels, speech bubbles, and vivid scenes that make language come alive.

We serve absolute beginners through advanced learners. For Latin, our videos are narrated in Latin with friendly English support; for ESL, they’re in clear English. Each lesson follows a book-like sequence and uses the natural method—learn by context, not cramming. Every video includes an interactive multiple-choice quiz so you can check understanding right away.

All videos and quizzes are free for everyone. If you’d like to go further, we offer companion e-books and print editions in the same graphic-novel format on Amazon Kindle. You can also support our work on Patreon, and first-time email subscribers get alerts for monthly free Kindle e-book offers plus other updates.

We also champion new independent authors on our platform—especially original stories written in Latin and works on Mythology and the Classical world for learners. We’ll gladly consider Tolkien-style fantasy, and even romance and political satire, when they harmonize with our theme.

Join us—read, watch, and learn in panels and pages.
